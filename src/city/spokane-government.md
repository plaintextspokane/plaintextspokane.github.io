**Spokane's Governments**

Spokane may refer to the city or the county of the same name - both have their own goverenance. 

**Spokane County**

The County of Spokane is governed by the Board of County Commissioners.  

**City of Spokane**

The City of Spokane is governed via a Mayor-Council system. 
 
In this system, the Mayor's office acts similar to an Executive branch of governmnet - like a State's Governor or Country's President.

See the current Mayor's page on the city's site <a href="https://my.spokanecity.org/mayor/" target="_blank">here ↑</a>

The City Council acts as the Leglislative branch of the city government.  
Spokane's City Council consists of 7 members: 
* 1 Council Presidient who is elected at-large and 
* 6 Council members: 2 from each of the City's 3 districts.

See current City Council members on the city's website <a href="https://my.spokanecity.org/citycouncil/members/" target="_blank">here ↑</a>
