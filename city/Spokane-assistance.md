There are ways to contact the Spokane police for non-emergency situations.

Some key numbers to know: 
* Emergency Services: 911
* Mental Health Crisis: 988
* Crime Check: 509-456-2233 Non-emergency police services
* City Services: 311 Non-emergency reporting of potholes, traffic signal issues, general information, etc.
* Social Services: 211 Social services and community resources like housing, food, and employment help
