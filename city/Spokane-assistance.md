**Spokane Assistance**

There are ways to contact the Spokane police for non-emergency situations.

<a href="https://my.spokanecity.org/police/contact/" target="_blank">Spokane Police Contact Information â†‘</a>

Some key numbers to know: 
* Emergency Services: 911
* Mental Health Crisis: 988
* Crime Check: 509-456-2233 Non-emergency police services
* City Services: 311 Non-emergency reporting of potholes, traffic signal issues, general information, etc. The city also has an <a href="https://myspokane311.my.site.com/citizen/s/" target="_blank">online 311 system</a> where you can report issues, request service, and ask questions. You can also download a MySpokane 311 app some official App stores too.
* Social Services: 211 Social services and community resources like housing, food, and employment help
